CTUS Switcher 8Ball

Answer the following questions to choose the switcher thats right for your show

 Is your show in HD/3G or 12G?

HD = 1080i, 3G = 1080p, 12G = 2160p

HD / 3G   12G  

Do you require a built-in router with the switcher?

If you have a separate router, such as a BM 40x40, an included switcher router may not be needed. This should only be for shows where multiple pathways are required. 

  Yes, router please  No included router needed

How many inputs do you need?

Be sure to include all cameras, graphics sources, character generators, and key / fill sources as separate inputs. All inputs are SDI.

Less than 16Between 16 and 22More than 22

How many M.E.s do you need?

Are you cutting for a single destination? Or do you need to provide separate camera cuts for different feeds, such as IMAG vs Broadcast

Note: a "clean feed" with no keys on is not a separate M.E. 

Only 12 or 34 or 5

Does your show require a complex, multi-box look?

Do you need to have multiple "pips" up at the same time, such as smaller windows of people talking or product shots over a background?

NoYesUnknown

Do you require Dante or MADI for multi-channel audio?

If just a stereo program audio embed is needed, a standard SDI audio embedder may be a simpler solution. This should only be a necessity for multi-track embedding and breakaway routing to different destinations, such as recording multiple languages on multiple SDI channels.

No, not neededYes, Dante or MADI
